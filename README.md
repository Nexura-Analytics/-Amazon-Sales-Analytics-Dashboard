📊 Amazon Sales Analytics Dashboard

Turning raw advertising data into clear business decisions.

This project is a data analytics dashboard built to analyze Amazon store performance.
The goal is simple: help sellers understand what is working, what is wasting money, and where growth opportunities exist.

When my friend started running his Amazon store, he had access to lots of data — impressions, clicks, orders, keywords, campaigns — but no clear story behind the numbers.

This dashboard transforms scattered metrics into a single, visual story of store performance.

📊 Performance Metrics
📉 CTR — Click Through Rate

CTR measures how attractive your ad is.

CTR = Clicks / Impressions

Higher CTR means:

✅ Customers find the ad relevant
✅ Product listing and keywords are effective

Low CTR suggests:

⚠ Weak ad copy
⚠ Poor keyword targeting

💰 ACOS — Advertising Cost of Sales

ACOS measures how much you spend to generate sales.

ACOS = Ad Spend / Sales

Lower ACOS generally means more efficient advertising.

Example:

ACOS = 20% → Very efficient

ACOS = 80% → Possibly overspending

🚀 ROAS — Return on Ad Spend

ROAS measures revenue generated per dollar spent.

ROAS = Sales / Ad Spend

Higher ROAS means:

📈 Better advertising profitability.
